Who Am I?

My name is Arjun Birdi, I am a graduating 4th year Electrical Engineering Student from Carleton University. Although I may be green on the outside, my side projects and constant pursuit of my interests have given me extensive knowledge and applied skills in various areas.

Throughout my university career, I have participated in several extracurricular groups, the most notable being the IEEE Carleton Student Branch.
